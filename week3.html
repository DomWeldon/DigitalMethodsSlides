Exercise: Research Design

In groups of three, install a research browser on your computer and come up with a topic of interest you wish to study. Compare the search between two national google pages and prepare a three minute presentation of your results.

You'll be up at the front of the room, ensure your talk has an introduction to your research question, a description of your method, and a summary of your findings. You will also need to evaluate how successful your project was.
