Exercise: Telling the History of a Webpage

In groups of three or more, for the remainder of the seminar, you should use the Interet Archive to explore the history of a website or webpage.

Think not just about telling a history of the site, but devise a research question or set of questions which you could look to answer using this style of research.

Be prepared to answer 3 questions as a group after you have done your initial research. I'll be coming around to help with any questions you have.

Go!

  • What topic did you pick and why is it important? What are your research questions?
  • Which website(s) did you look at and why? What did you find?
  • Can you answer your research questions based on any findings? How effective is this method at addressing your RQs?
